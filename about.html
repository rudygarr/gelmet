About

Gelmet is basically a home for the way I solve problems.

It is part studio, part systems lab, part production brain, and part place to put ideas that are too useful to ignore.

What Gelmet exists to do

Build things that solve real problems and make other people's jobs easier. Sometimes that is software. Sometimes it is consulting, installs, training, or AI-supported systems. The instinct behind all of it is the same: find the real problem, remove friction, and leave things better than they were before.

What carries over

  • Question assumptions first
  • Technology should adapt to people
  • Simple is not the same as shallow
  • Reliable beats impressive
  • Leadership means removing obstacles for other people
Gelmet is broad on purpose because the mission is bigger than one app or one lane.
