What Gelmet is

A studio for useful tools, stronger systems, and less friction.

Gelmet is built around practical creativity. It is for rooms that need to run better, teams that need more clarity, and ideas that need to become something real. The studio is intentionally broad, but the standard stays narrow: if it is not useful, it does not belong.

Apps

Purpose-built software for live production, organization, and real-world workflows that do not fit generic tools very well.

Consulting

Production support, installs, systems thinking, and training grounded in hands-on experience instead of abstract advice.

AI

Thoughtful AI help for communication, process, organization, and creative work without the usual hype fog.

Featured apps

Software is one way the studio solves problems.

The strongest product work at Gelmet starts with a real pain point, a real room, and a real person who needs the tool to make sense quickly.

Spot Projector

A projector-based follow spot app for schools, churches, community theaters, and smaller venues.

iPhone + iPad

Spot Projector exists for rooms that need a simple spotlight solution without the complexity, storage, and staffing requirements of a traditional follow spot. It is practical, fast to understand, and intentionally built for the kinds of teams that do not have time for a giant learning curve.

SpotMover

A moving-light follow spot control concept designed to make advanced fixtures more approachable.

TestFlight

SpotMover is about re-imagining the follow spot around fixtures many venues already own. Instead of pretending everyone wants a full lighting-console mindset, the goal is to make moving-light control feel clear enough that more rooms can actually use it.

How Gelmet works

Useful technology over impressive-looking technology.

Gelmet is not built around complexity for its own sake. It is built around clearer workflows, better choices, and systems people can actually live with. The goal is not to flex technology. It is to help people do better work with less friction and more confidence.

What that looks like
  • Build for real rooms, not imaginary perfect ones
  • Make hard things feel clearer without dumbing them down
  • Design for reliability before cleverness
  • Keep personality without sacrificing professionalism
  • Leave people and teams stronger than you found them
Where this is headed

One studio. Multiple tools. Same instinct behind all of them.

Gelmet is growing into a home for apps, consulting, live production problem-solving, and thoughtful AI systems. Some projects are already useful. Some are still sharpening. The throughline is simple: find the real problem, build the right support around it, and make the end result feel more human than the process that created it.
