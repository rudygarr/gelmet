What Gelmet is

A studio built around solving weird production and technology problems.

I am not interested in technology for its own sake. I care about things running better, people feeling less overwhelmed, and systems making more sense. Gelmet is broad on purpose, but the filter is simple: if it does not make someone else's job easier, it does not belong here.

Featured apps

Software is one of the ways I try to fix what is annoying.

The best Gelmet app ideas usually start when a workflow feels clunky, overpriced, outdated, or way more complicated than it needs to be.

Spot Projector

A projector-based follow spot app for schools, churches, community theaters, and smaller venues.

iPhone + iPad
Now testing in real venues Built for fast setup Made for non-specialists

Spot Projector came from a simple question: if a room already has a projector, why should a follow spot solution still feel so expensive, bulky, or overcomplicated? It is built to be practical, fast to learn, and realistic for the kinds of teams that do not have a specialist for everything.

SpotMover

A moving-light follow spot control concept designed to make advanced fixtures more approachable.

TestFlight
Standalone first Lighting workflow focused Built around follow spot logic

SpotMover is about rethinking the follow spot around moving lights many venues already own. Instead of assuming everyone wants to think like a lighting programmer, the goal is to make powerful fixtures feel approachable enough that more rooms can actually use them well.

How Gelmet works

Useful beats impressive every time.

I do not care how clever a system sounds if it makes people miserable to use. Gelmet is about clearer workflows, better decisions, and tools that help instead of showing off. If the technology becomes the star of the conversation, something probably went wrong.

What that looks like
  • Start with the real problem, not the flashy solution
  • Build for real rooms, budgets, and teams
  • Make hard things clearer without making people feel dumb
  • Choose reliability before cleverness
  • Leave people and teams stronger than you found them
Where this is headed

One studio. Different kinds of work. Same brain behind all of it.

Gelmet is becoming a home for apps, consulting, production problem-solving, and AI systems that actually help. Some of it is already live. Some of it is still taking shape. The throughline is simple: question assumptions, remove friction, build something better, and teach people how to use it without making them feel overwhelmed.
